Privacy Policy

At AU English IT Courses, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and how we safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your IP address and browser type
  • Any other information you provide through forms

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide details about our English and IT courses
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support
  • Analyze website performance and user behavior
  • Improve the quality of our courses and services

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • With third-party service providers who assist us under strict data protection agreements

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

5. Data Security

We store your personal data on secure servers with access limited to authorized staff only. All data transmission is encrypted using SSL protocols.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Know what data we collect
  • Request corrections to your personal data
  • Request the deletion of your data
  • Object to the processing of your data

You can contact us to exercise any of these rights using the contact information provided on our website.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Please check this page regularly for the latest information.
