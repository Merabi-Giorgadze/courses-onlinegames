Privacy Policy

At AU Poker Courses, we value the privacy of our users' personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your data.

1. Collected Information

When you use our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your IP address and browser information
  • Any other information you provide through forms

2. Use of Information

The data we collect may be used for the following purposes:

  • To provide information about our courses
  • To respond to your queries and provide support
  • To conduct statistical analysis
  • To improve the quality of our services

3. Sharing with Third Parties

Your personal data will only be shared with third parties in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To fulfill our legal obligations
  • With our service providers, under contractual agreements

4. Cookies

We use cookies on our website to enhance the user experience. For more details on cookies, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

5. Data Security

Your personal data is stored on secure servers and can only be accessed by authorized personnel. Data transmission is encrypted with SSL certification.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • To learn what data is being collected
  • To request correction of your data
  • To request deletion of your data
  • To object to the processing of your data

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at the provided address.

7. Changes

Our Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
