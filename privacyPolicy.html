Privacy Policy

At AU Online Gaming Courses, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe while you learn and explore with us.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for our gaming courses or interact with our website, we may collect the following details:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your IP address and browser information
  • Your course preferences and selected modules
  • Any additional details you submit via contact or registration forms

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide access to our online gaming courses and resources
  • Personalise your learning experience
  • Communicate important updates or support
  • Monitor course performance and user engagement
  • Improve our services, platform features, and curriculum

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations in Australia

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve functionality and understand how users interact with our platform. These cookies help us provide a smoother learning experience. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

5. Data Security

All personal information is securely stored on encrypted servers with restricted access. We apply SSL encryption for data transmission and follow industry best practices to keep your information safe from unauthorised access.

6. Your Rights

Under Australian privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request corrections if your data is inaccurate
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for marketing or promotional communication

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the information provided on our website.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements or legal updates. Please revisit this page periodically to stay informed.
