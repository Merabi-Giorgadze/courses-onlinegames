Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how AU English IT Courses uses cookies on its website to improve user experience and ensure proper functionality.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences to enhance your browsing experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly.

Performance Cookies: Help us analyze how users interact with our website.

Functionality Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences for a more personalized experience.

Marketing Cookies: Deliver relevant advertising and track the effectiveness of our campaigns.

3. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some parts of the website may not function correctly if cookies are disabled.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics or social media pixels to collect statistical and marketing data.

5. Cookie Duration

Some cookies expire when you close your browser (session cookies), while others remain on your device until manually deleted or expired (persistent cookies).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data. Please contact us if you have any concerns regarding your data or cookie usage.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Please check back periodically for the latest version.
