Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how AU Poker Academy uses cookies on its website to ensure a better user experience.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They help remember preferences, improve site efficiency, and enhance your experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Necessary Cookies: Essential for the proper functionality of the website.

Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.

Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site.

Marketing Cookies: Provide content and ads tailored to your interests.

3. Managing Your Cookies

You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies. However, doing so may affect your experience on our site.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel for analytics and marketing purposes.

5. Cookie Retention

Session cookies are deleted when your browser is closed. Persistent cookies may remain for a set duration.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access and control your personal data. Contact us for any concerns or requests.

7. Updates to this Policy

This cookie policy may be updated periodically. Please review this page regularly for any changes.
